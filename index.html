Techthronix Performance Analysis

This project was a unique one for Techthronix. The main aim of this analysis is to gain insights into Techtronix's performance over the past fiscal year using the data provided by the company. By analyzing the data, the project identified strengths, weaknesses, and opportunities for improvement across various aspects of the business. The comprehensive suggestions outlined in the project helped Techtronix to effectively tackle the underperformance observed in certain product segments and recommended opportunities for market expansion in other regions like Europe, NorthAmerica and Asia pacific. This analysis was performed using Microsoft Power BI

User Experience (UX) Analysis for Flex Trade app using Microsoft Excel

This client needed help in investigating its User-engagement within the app. While the conversion rate is high at 95%, indicating successful actions and transactions, the bounce rate is also seemingly high at 49%. What this means in simple term is that, a significant portion of users leave immediately after only one screen. After thorough analysis, it was discovered that the checkout process needed to be streamlined to reduce chart abandonement. The search functionality also needed enhancement to help users easily find what they are looking for .
